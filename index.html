About Us

The UNEP Adaptation Gap Report 2016 warns of increasing impacts and resulting increases in global adaptation costs by 2030 or 2050 that will likely be much higher than currently expected. It is necessary to quantify the risk due to extreme climatic events. The e-CREAT (Extreme Climate Risk Estimation and Assessment Tool) provides a platform for information on natural hazard risk, disaster risk analysis and communication. The e-CREAT is a comprehensive risk model and will be utilized for the probabilistic assessment of natural hazards. We intend to develop e-CREAT as a model for risk calculations based on hazard, exposure and physical vulnerability data. Through hazard mapping, risk assessment and cost-benefit analysis tools, the platform is expected to allow the user to determine conjoint or cascading risk on an inter-related multi-hazard basis distinguishing the platform from previous single hazard analyses. The product can be utilized by individuals/organization/institutions/companies to assesses catastrophe exposure, estimate losses and design reinsurance programs to efficiently manage net risks due to extreme climatic events.
